About Domicile Certificate

A Domicile Certificate is an official Indian document proving you're a permanent resident of a specific state/UT, essential for education, jobs, and government benefits, issued by officials like Tehsildars/DMs, requiring proof of long-term residence (often 15-20 years, varying by state) and applying through local revenue departments.

Key Uses: Education, Employment, Government Schemes, Legal.

Eligibility: Living in state for significant period, apply via local revenue authorities or online portal.

Documents Required: Ration Card, Property Records, Utility Bills, Education Certificates, Voter Lists, etc.

For minors, parents/guardians can apply on their behalf. Validity often lifetime but check for recent requirements (1-3 years).
